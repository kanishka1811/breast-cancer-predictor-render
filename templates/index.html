Breast Cancer Predictor

Aimed at spreading awareness, it predicts whether cancer is
Benign or Malignant.

Benign tumors tend to grow slowly and do not spread.

Malignant tumors can grow rapidly, invade and destroy nearby normal
tissues, and spread throughout the body.

The predictor uses the "K-Nearest Neighbors" machine learning algorithm and
has an accuracy of 97.85%.

However, it is recommended that you refer to a medical report before reaching any conclusions.
